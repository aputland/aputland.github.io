Protective Ventilation Calculator

During anaesthesia the prevalence of pulmonary complications may be reduced by limiting tidal volumes to between 6 and 8 ml/kg, ideal body weight. In practical terms, it can be difficult to estimate the ideal body weight and hence the best tidal volume required for an individual patient. This application is recommended for healthcare professionals and is only applicable to adult patients with heights greater than 153cm.
