Protective Ventilation Calculator


Introduction

In general anaesthesia, with the use of mechanical ventilation, the prevalence of pulmonary complications may be reduced by limiting tidal volumes to between 6 and 8 ml/kg, using the ideal body weight. In practical terms, it can be difficult to estimate the ideal body weight and hence the best tidal volume required for an individual patient. This software is designed to calculate a recommended range of tidal volumes based on a calculation of ideal body weight made using the height and gender.

The calculation is as follows:

Men   50 + 0.91 x (height in cm - 152.4) ml

Women   45.5 + 0.91 x (height in cm - 152.4) ml

Disclaimer

This application is recommended only for healthcare professionals, experienced in adminstering general anaesthesia, and is only applicable to adult patients with heights above 152cm. This tool should not be used to replace the professional responsibility to ensure that anaesthetised patients are ventilated safely and must be used under the supervision of an appropriately qualified professional.
